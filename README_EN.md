## 🎯 Project Overview

**Wild Chicken Private University (WCU)** is an open-source virtual university project that satirizes the monopoly of educational resources while providing tech enthusiasts with an experimental .edu email platform.

In an era where educational resources are monopolized, we believe everyone deserves their own .edu email—it's not just an email, it's a key to student discounts, free software, and academic resources.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer**: This is not a real university. For entertainment and educational purposes only.
